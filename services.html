Our Services

ARCHITECTURE DESIGN AND TURNKEY SERVICES

Bringing the avant-garde tradition in the designing concept, we at BALAJI INTERIORS provide residential, commercial, hospitality and industrial services to our clients with a lot of innovations as well as creativity. We help in providing the materials as well as contractors, vendors and helps in building your structure from scratch to the completion.

INTERIOR DESIGN AND TURNKEY SERVICES

Its the interiors that define a perfect home/office these days. One may have large properties, huge buildings, constructed houses, and commercial space, but it is the interiors that provide and add an asset to the projects. We at BALAJI INTERIORS make interior spaces functional, safe, and beautiful by determining space requirements and selecting decorative items, such as colors, lighting, and materials. We work on concepts. Our design is a reflection of our expertise and clients’ requirements.
We are committed to supporting you at every stage of your project with comprehensive resources for design and project coordination. We take on full responsibility for budget, schedule management, and all project-related elements.
In interior designing projects, we also work on a turnkey basis, we undertake complete projects wherein the entire scope of work including material, labor, designing & execution is handled by us.
Client involvement is still critical to the success of a Design-Build project, particularly for providing requirements, checking designs and materials, and final product acceptance. Youll be informed and consulted throughout the process without having a project workload on your shoulder.

LANDSCAPE ARCHITECTURE

We offer a wide range of Landscaping services to enhance your landscaping needs for your home or business. We have the expertise and knowledge to design, installation, and maintaining quality landscapes. Part of your landscape needs may include terrace, garden, patios, sidewalks, driveways, pool decks, and outdoor living area and many more unused spaces. We dedicate ourselves to providing an environment that is customized to meet your specific needs and interests.
